FAQs Section

Q: The Wi-Fi seems slow what can I do?

First, try this: Disconnect and reconnect to the Wi-Fi network Toggle your devices Airplane Mode on and off Move closer to the main router (usually in the living room) Avoid streaming or downloading on multiple devices at once this may affect speed.

Q: Is there a backup connection or hotspot?

Yes in case of local outages or service issues, we sometimes provide a backup hotspot device (availability may vary). Message us if the main Wi-Fi is completely down.

Q: Can I work remotely from here?

Absolutely! The home is equipped with reliable high-speed internet and a cozy workspace. Great for video calls, emails, and Netflix breaks

Q: Alexa or smart devices arent responding why?

If Alexa or other smart home gadgets aren't working, they may have lost connection. Please check the Wi-Fi on your own device if its down, those devices likely are too.

House Rules

Respecting Nature & Neighbors

House Rules So Everyone Feels at Home

Max 5 Guests Allowed Per City Permit

Our space is designed to comfortably host up to 5 guests. Please dont exceed this; it keeps things safe, legal, and enjoyable for all.

Up to 3 Vehicles Parking Limit

You're welcome to park up to 3 vehicles on the property. If you have more guests arriving separately, consider carpooling the city has set limits, and we appreciate your help in following them.

Quiet Hours (Peace is Golden) : 10 PM 7 AM

Out of respect for our neighbors, please keep noise to a minimum between 10:00 PM and 7:00 AM. Think of it as a time for winding down, stargazing, or soft background music.

Respect the Neighborhood No Nuisance Rules

Please avoid loud noise, improper parking, or disruptive behavior. These are classified as Public & Private Nuisance by the city.

Cleanliness & Care

Trash & Recycling Black (Trash), Blue (Recycle)

Youll find the bins near the front-side of the house. Help us keep things clean by using the correct bin for your waste.

No Smoking Indoors Outdoor Only

To keep the air fresh for everyone, please refrain from smoking indoors. Outdoor smoking is fine just dispose responsibly.

No Food or Drinks in Bedrooms (Waters Okay)

To avoid spills or stains, please enjoy food in the dining or kitchen areas only.

Short blackouts can happen. Verify other houses nearby also lost power. => Look for a main breaker box (often in hallway or utility area) If needed, call your host for guidance

Water or Plumbing Emergency
